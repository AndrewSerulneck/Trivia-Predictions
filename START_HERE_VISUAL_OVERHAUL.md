## 🎨 The Design System (Quick Reference)

### Colors
- **Surfaces:** #020617 (canvas), #0f172a (surface), #1e293b (elevated)
- **Text:** #f8fafc (primary), #e2e8f0 (secondary), #94a3b8 (muted)
- **Accents:** Cyan #06b6d4, Amber #f59e0b, Rose #f43f5e
- **Game Gradients:**
  - Live Trivia: cyan → blue → violet
  - Bingo: green + **CYAN border** (NOT warm orange)
  - Pick'Em: navy ↔ magenta split
  - Fantasy: dark forest

### Typography
- **Display:** Bree Serif, 44px, uppercase
- **H1/H2:** Bree Serif, 36px/30px
- **Body:** Nunito 600, 16px [DEFAULT]
- **Eyebrow:** Nunito 900, 11px, uppercase, tracked

### Spacing (4px grid)
- 1: 4px | 2: 8px | 3: 12px | 4: 16px | 6: 24px | 8: 32px
